Zero Energy Cooling Chamber

This program allows you to create a Zero Energy Cooling Chamber with your chosen specified dimensions, material and location for the chamber.

You can choose whether or not to use temperature data from a yearly time scale or a 24 hour time scale. As you adjust variable values, the graphs in the "Heat Transfer & Water" tab will update. To figure out how much water is necessary and the cost of your chamber, simply press the "Calculate" button, you can do this as many times as is desired. Ultimately you want to create a chamber that is the most cost efficient, while still being able to store enough food.

Infectious Disease Modeling

SEIR Model: Susceptible-Exposed-Infected_Recovered

This model allows you to see how different infectious disease outbreaks occur. By implementing different mitigation strategies you can see how useful and beneficial certain strategies are. You can also see how different age groups are effcted differently. Addittionaly, you can look at data from specific historical epidemics and pandemics.
